# 000. ADR Template

## Status
ACCEPTED

## Context
We need a standard way to document architectural decisions to ensure history is preserved and decisions are justifiable.

## Decision
We will use Architecture Decision Records (ADR) to document significant architectural changes.

## Structure
Each ADR will follow this structure:

### Title
Number and short description (e.g., `001-use-fastapi.md`)

### Status
One of: PROPOSED, ACCEPTED, REJECTED, DEPRECATED.

### Context
What is the problem? What are the constraints? Why is this decision needed?

### Decision
What did we decide?

### Consequences
What becomes easier? What becomes harder? What are the limitations or risks?
